About this Blog:

Bringing magic to life...

I decided to start this blog as a means of getting my writings and ideas out there into the world... first off, Im not what one would call your "typical" human, I believe in the strange and magical. I believe in miracles and unconditional love. I believe in things that most people would not even dare to dream about. I know these views are not always widely accepted, but Im hoping that maybe, this will get out there to the people who are like myself :) or better yet, help others who might need a little escape from the mundane activities of every day life, find a little magic to put into their day, too.
